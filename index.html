📁 entry 1.txt

June 2nd

Dear diary,


I was reminiscing about the times I would sit in my big grey tub full of toys when I was little. It was a huge Rubbermaid plastic tub. The kind you would store important stuff in. However, this wasn't any ordinary tub. It was my treasure chest.


I would literally sit on top of the toys. The big toy box was located in my closet and sometimes I would even close the closet door while sitting on the toys.


Not sure what was going through my mind when doing this because it kinda hurt sitting on them, but I thought it was fun.


I also just remembered... the SHHHKKRRRHHRKKSSSS noise that would fill the bedroom whenever I dug through the toy box. Plastic would scrape against more plastic until my fingers laid on the specific toy I wanted to play with.

📁 entry 2.txt

June 5th

Dear diary,


I remember Owl City being a big part of my childhood. It's a faint memory, but I remember me and dad recording us singing Fireflies. At the time, I didn't know what the words meant, but the melody was catchy and I loved it.


Ever since then, I've been a huge fan. I still listen to their music every now and then, specifically Vanilla Twilight. Since me and dad are far apart, in different cities, I get emotional listening to it.


I wish I could share that moment with him again. Maybe with Vanilla Twilight this time.
